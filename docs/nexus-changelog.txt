Version 1.8.0

NEW
- Custom NPC Framework now supports forage loot tables. Content packs can give a
  custom/modded spouse their own forage loot (Common/Uncommon/Rare tiers + a
  Prismatic Shard jackpot line). This is the groundwork for the optional Stardew
  Valley Expanded spouse support pack.


Version 1.7.3

NEW
- Polyamory / multi-spouse compatibility (optional). For multi-spouse mods like
  Polyamory Sweet Love or Free Love, a new "Polyamory / Multi-Spouse
  Compatibility" option (off by default) gives all of your spouses the overhaul's
  morning greetings, not just your primary spouse. The other relationship systems
  (feeding, anniversary, divorce, jealousy, etc.) still apply to the primary
  spouse only.
- "Spouses Cook For You" compatibility (optional). A new option (off by default)
  makes the mod treat every day as a spouse cooking day, so it never asks you to
  leave food, never eats the meals that mod prepares, and never gives a hunger
  penalty. The spouse's cooking dialogue still plays.

FIXES
- Roommates (Krobus) are no longer forced into a romantic relationship. The mod
  was treating a roommate exactly like a spouse - romantic morning greetings,
  jealousy, anniversaries, and so on. Roommates now stay platonic with their
  normal dialogue. A new "Overhaul a Roommate (Krobus)" option (off by default)
  can turn the full overhaul back on for a roommate if you want it.
- "Spend quality time" spouse requests can now actually be completed by spending
  time together. Requests like "spend a little time with me at home" or "take a
  walk with me" previously could only be finished by handing over a gift. Talking
  to your spouse on the request day now completes them (giving any gift still
  works too).

Note: a roommate trying to share the bed is controlled by your roommate / multi-
partner mod or the base game, not by Marriage Overhaul, so it isn't affected by
this update.
